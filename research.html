Research standards

If it cannot be reconstructed, it is not institutional memory.

Each artifact should separate observation from assumption, preserve the surrounding context, and leave enough trace that a later reader can find the route.

Field Notes are allowed to be unfinished. They are not allowed to be vague. Recurring observations live in Field Notes; reconstructed evidence lives in Case Files; generalized lessons move into Working Papers.
